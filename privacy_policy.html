Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Introduction

Offline Fitness Workouts ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Offline Fitness Workouts is designed to work completely offline and does not transmit any data to external servers.

All workout data, favorites, and progress tracking are stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this information, and it never leaves your device.

2. Permissions

This application requires zero permissions. It does not request or use:

  • Camera access
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • External storage access
  • Phone/SMS access
  • Notification permissions
  • Any other device permissions

Our app is designed to function entirely without requesting any permissions from your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

3. Data Storage

All data generated by the app (workout history, favorites, progress) is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Your workout data remains completely private and under your control at all times.

4. Network Activity

This app makes zero network requests. It operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

You can use Offline Fitness Workouts anywhere, anytime, without any internet connection. All features work entirely offline.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • User tracking
  • Any third-party data collection services

We do not integrate with any external services that could collect or track your information.

6. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using standard SQLite encryption. We recommend:

  • Using device security features (PIN, fingerprint, etc.)
  • Keeping your device updated
  • Not sharing your device with unauthorized users

Since no data leaves your device, your information is protected by your device's own security measures.

7. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, this app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Our app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All data stored locally on your device can be removed by uninstalling the application.

You have complete control over your data at all times.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document and within the app. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will be revised accordingly.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing for Offline Fitness Workouts.

We are committed to transparency and will respond to any privacy-related inquiries promptly.

This app respects your privacy and operates with complete transparency.
