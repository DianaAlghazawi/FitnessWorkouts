Offline Fitness Workouts - Complete Workout Guide

A fully offline fitness application that provides comprehensive workout routines without requiring any internet connection or permissions.

KEY FEATURES:
- 5 workout categories with multiple routines
- Step-by-step exercise instructions
- Built-in workout timer
- Progress tracking (workouts completed, minutes trained, calories burned)
- Favorites system
- Difficulty filtering (Easy, Medium, Hard)
- Search functionality
- Light/Dark theme support
- 100% offline - no internet required
- Zero permissions - completely private

WORKOUT CATEGORIES:
1. Full Body Workouts - Complete body training routines
2. Abs & Core - Core strengthening exercises
3. Arms & Shoulders - Upper body workouts
4. Legs & Glutes - Lower body training
5. Stretching & Flexibility - Flexibility and recovery routines

EACH WORKOUT INCLUDES:
- Detailed description
- Duration in minutes
- Difficulty level
- Step-by-step instructions
- Estimated calories burned

PRIVACY & SECURITY:
- No data collection
- No permissions required
- All data stored locally on device
- No network requests
- No third-party tracking

Perfect for users who want a privacy-focused, offline fitness solution that works anywhere, anytime.
